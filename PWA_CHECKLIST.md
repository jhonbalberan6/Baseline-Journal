## ⚠️ KNOWN ISSUES & FIXES

**Issue: "Install button not showing"**
- Solution: Hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R)
- Reason: Older service worker cached

**Issue: "App blank after install"**
- Solution: Close/reopen app
- Reason: First load on phone needs service worker ready

**Issue: "Entries disappeared after update"**
- Solution: They're in IndexedDB (not lost!)
- Check: DevTools → Application → IndexedDB → BaselineJournal
